You don't need more clothes. You need a new way of seeing what you already have.

Micro-Styling is a focused, high-value session where Danielle builds three complete, styled looks using only what's already in your closet. No shopping trip. No new purchases. Just an expert eye applied to the pieces you already own — and the discovery that your wardrobe is probably more versatile than you've been giving it credit for.

This service is ideal for the moment when you're staring into a full closet and feeling like you have nothing to wear. It's also ideal if you're in a season of intentionality — building mindfully, not impulsively — and you want to maximize what you have before investing in anything new.

**What you'll get:**
- 3 complete, camera-ready outfits from your existing pieces
- Styling notes on how each look works and how to adapt it
- Insights into gaps in your wardrobe (if any) that are worth filling
- A refreshed perspective on pieces you may have been overlooking

Micro-Styling sessions can be done in-person or virtually (you'll walk through your closet on camera). Each session focuses on a specific context — workwear, casual/weekend, date nights, or whatever you need most.

Sessions can be repeated as needed. Each one builds on the last.
