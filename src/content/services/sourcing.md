Some clients don't want to shop. They want it handled.

Sourcing and Personal Shopping is Danielle's white-glove service for clients who want expert curation without the time investment. You tell her what you need — a specific occasion outfit, a capsule wardrobe for a new role, a complete closet overhaul — and she finds it. Every piece is selected with your color palette, body proportions, lifestyle needs, and style archetype in mind.

This isn't browsing. It's precision targeting.

Danielle's sourcing process involves identifying specific items across price points and brands, presenting curated options for client approval, and handling the logistics of pulling pieces for try-on sessions or coordinating returns. Whether you need one killer blazer or a complete seasonal wardrobe, sourcing removes the friction from building a wardrobe you love.

**How it works:**
- Initial briefing call (included in consultation): review your needs, budget, timeline, and priorities
- Danielle researches, pulls, and presents curated options
- You review, approve, and purchase your selections
- A finder's fee (~15% of the total purchase) applies to sourced items

This service is especially popular with VIP clients, busy executives, new parents, and anyone navigating a major wardrobe rebuild who values their time.

Sourcing can be done as a standalone service or as an ongoing component of a VIP Styling relationship.
