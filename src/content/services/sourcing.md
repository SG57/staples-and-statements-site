Some clients don't want to shop. They want it handled.

Sourcing and Personal Shopping is my white-glove service for clients who want expert curation without the time commitment. You describe what you need — a standout outfit for a specific event, a capsule wardrobe for a new role or a full wardrobe refresh — and I source it. Every piece is selected with your color palette, body proportions, lifestyle needs, and style archetype in mind.

This isn't browsing. It's precision.

My sourcing process involves identifying highly specific pieces across brands and price points, presenting curated options for client approval, and managing the logistics of pulling items for try-on sessions or researching return policies. Whether you need one exceptional blazer or an entire seasonal wardrobe, sourcing removes the friction from building a closet you truly love.

**What You'll Receive:**
- Initial briefing call (included in consultation) to review your needs, budget, timeline, and priorities
- I research, pull and present curated options
- You review, approve, and purchase your selections
- A finder's fee (approximately 15% of the total purchase) applies to one-off sourced items

This service is especially popular with VIP clients, busy executives, new parents and anyone navigating a significant wardrobe transition who values their time.

Sourcing can be booked as a standalone service or as an ongoing component of a VIP Styling relationship.
