Every great wardrobe begins with a conversation.

Before we dive into your closet, your colors, or your shopping habits, we start with you — who you are, where you're going, and what you want your style to say when you walk into a room. This is a relaxed, no-pressure consultation designed to help clarify what kind of support will serve you best.

In just 20 minutes, we'll talk through your current style challenges, your goals (whether that's a new job, a life transition, or simply wanting to feel more like yourself again), and which of my services will make the most sense for where you are right now.

**There's no upsell here — only honest guidance.** If the best place to start is a Color Analysis, that's what I'll recommend. If a Wardrobe Revamp would serve you better, I'll say so. The goal is simply to ensure your investment goes exactly where it should.

The consultation can take place over the phone, Zoom or FaceTime — wherever you're most comfortable. No preparation required. Just come as you are.
