You don't need more clothes. You need a closet that makes sense.

A Closet Clean Out is a hands-on session where Danielle goes through your wardrobe piece by piece — editing what no longer serves you, rediscovering what you've been overlooking, and reorganizing what remains into a functional, intentional system.

This isn't about throwing everything away. It's about clarity. Most clients are surprised to find they already own a stronger wardrobe than they thought — it was just buried under pieces that don't fit, don't flatter, or don't align with who they are now.

**What you'll get:**
- A full wardrobe audit — every piece evaluated for fit, condition, relevance, and style alignment
- Clear keep / donate / alter categories
- A reorganized closet built around how you actually live and dress
- A gap analysis: what's missing and what to invest in next
- Styling notes on pieces you're keeping — new ways to wear what you already own

A Closet Clean Out pairs beautifully with the Master Style Moodboard — once your closet is edited, the moodboard becomes your roadmap for building it back up with intention.

Sessions are $55/hr and can be done in-person or virtually. Most closet clean outs take 2–4 hours depending on wardrobe size.
