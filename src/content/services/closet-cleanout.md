You don't need more clothes. You need a closet that makes sense.

A Closet Clean Out is a hands-on session where I work through your wardrobe piece by piece — editing what no longer serves you, rediscovering what's been overlooked and reorganizing what remains into a functional, intentional system.

This process isn't about throwing everything away. It's about clarity. Many clients are surprised to realize they already own a stronger wardrobe than they thought — it was simply buried beneath pieces that don't fit, don't flatter, or no longer reflect who they are today.

**What You'll Receive:**
- A full wardrobe audit, with every piece evaluated for fit, condition, relevance, and style alignment
- Clear keep, donate and alter categories
- A reorganized closet built around how you actually live and dress
- A gap analysis outlining what's missing and what to invest in next
- Styling notes for pieces you're keeping — including new ways to wear what you already own

A Closet Clean Out pairs beautifully with the Master Style Moodboard. Once your wardrobe has been edited, the moodboard becomes your roadmap for rebuilding it with clarity and intention.

Sessions are $55/hr and can be done either in person or virtually. Most closet clean outs take between 2-4 hours, depending on the size of the wardrobe.
