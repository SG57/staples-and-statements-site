Every body is a good body for great style. But every body has its own geometry — and the most flattering wardrobe is the one built around your actual proportions, not a generic size chart or someone else's body type.

Body Analysis is about understanding the visual relationship between your natural shape and the clothing you put on it. Danielle uses a trained eye and a practical framework to identify your natural proportions, your visual balance points, and the silhouettes, cuts, and styling details that will consistently work in your favor.

This isn't about hiding or minimizing. It's about dressing with intention — understanding why some outfits feel effortless and others never quite land, even when everything is technically "in your size."

**What you'll get:**
- Clear identification of your natural body proportions
- Silhouettes and cuts that create your most balanced, elevated look
- Specific styling details to embrace (and a few to retire)
- A practical lens for evaluating everything in your closet going forward

Body Analysis pairs beautifully with Color Analysis — together, they give you a complete style foundation. Many clients book both in the same session or on back-to-back calls.
