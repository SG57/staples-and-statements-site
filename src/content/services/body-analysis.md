Every body is worthy of great style. But every body has its own unique geometry — and the most flattering wardrobe is the one designed around your natural proportions, not a generic size chart or someone else's body type.

Body Analysis focuses on understanding the visual relationship between your natural shape and the clothing you put on it. Using a trained eye and a practical framework, I identify your natural proportions, your visual balance points and the silhouettes, cuts and styling details that consistently work in your favor.

This isn't about hiding or minimizing your body. It's about dressing with intention — understanding why some outfits feel effortlessly right while others never quite land, even when everything is technically "your size."

**What You'll Receive:**
- Clear identification of your natural body proportions
- Silhouettes and cuts that create your most balanced, elevated look
- Specific styling details to embrace (and a few to retire)
- A practical framework for evaluating everything in your wardrobe moving forward

Body Analysis pairs beautifully with Color Analysis — together, they create a complete foundation for your personal style. Many clients choose to book both services in the same session or on back-to-back calls.
