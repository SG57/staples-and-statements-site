Some wardrobes need more than fresh eyes. They need a complete overhaul.

The Wardrobe Revamp is Danielle's signature in-depth service: a full edit of your current closet, a ruthless audit of what stays and what goes, and a strategic plan for filling the gaps with intentional pieces that actually serve your life. It's not just about getting organized. It's about building a wardrobe that works — one that makes getting dressed the easiest part of your day.

This service is especially powerful during life transitions: a career change, a new relationship, post-pregnancy, weight changes, relocating to a new city, stepping into a more public role. When your life shifts, your wardrobe needs to catch up.

**What we'll cover:**
- Full closet edit and audit (keep, donate, consign, alter, discard)
- Identification of your core wardrobe needs vs. impulse buys
- Organization and styling of your edited closet
- A curated shopping list of strategic pieces to fill real gaps
- Styling of new purchase combinations as items are added

Sessions are done in-person at your home or location of choice. Travel included within San Diego. Range fee applies for locations 30+ miles from San Diego.

The hourly rate reflects the depth of focus and the lasting impact — most clients find their wardrobe decisions dramatically simplified for months afterward.
