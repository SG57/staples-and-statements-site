The Muse lives where romance and femininity intersect. Your wardrobe is an expression of your inner world — layered, romantic, with a bit of magic to it. You're drawn to flowing silhouettes, bold feminine prints, fun and ornate details, and fabrics that move. Florals, lace, soft watercolor tones, bright dreamy pops of color and unexpected feminine details like sequins and feathers feel instinctively right to you.

Your style says: *I see beauty everywhere I go, and I wear it.*

**Signature pieces:** Flowy midi dresses, romantic blouses, feminine jewelry, statement accessories, floral prints, ethereal layering.

**Your palette:** Blush pink, peach, lavender, champagne, ivory, sage green, misty blue

**Muse icons:** Carrie Bradshaw, Alexa Chung, Zooey Deschanel.
