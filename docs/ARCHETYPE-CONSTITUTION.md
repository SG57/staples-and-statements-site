## Preamble

The archetype system represents your **energetic blueprint** — how you express your identity emotionally, aesthetically, and energetically. Each archetype holds clues about:

- Your **natural style essence**
- What clothing you feel **most powerful** in
- Your **intuitive color palette**
- **Textures, shapes, and silhouettes** that mirror your inner energy
- How to style yourself in a way that feels **aligned and not forced**

These are Danielle's fashion-focused adaptations of traditional archetypes, designed to be actionable wardrobe guidance rather than abstract personality types.

---

## Gender Framework

- **Feminine archetypes:** 7 types — feminine energy tends to **diversify**
- **Masculine archetypes:** 5 types — masculine energy tends to **consolidate**
- Gender identification does NOT determine which system applies — anyone can fall anywhere on the spectrum
- Masculine archetypes can be described as: **directional, expressive, assertive, grounded, and expansive**
- This is a **parallel framework** — both systems coexist and a person may explore both

## Masculine Archetypes

### 1. The Protector (Letter A)

| Attribute | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| **Energy** | Grounded, capable, ready for whatever the day brings |
| **Presence** | Steady, grounding, reassuring |
| **Confidence Signal** | Capability, reliability, quiet strength |
| **Style Identity** | Steady and rooted — knows what works and returns to it |
| **Shopping Pull** | Quality, durability, pieces that feel dependable over time |
| **Trend Relationship** | Mostly indifferent — prefers timelessness over novelty |
| **Style Changes Because** | Adapts to environment; dresses for reliability + function |

> Values stability, reliability, and quiet strength. Presence feels grounding and capable. Style reflects a desire to be prepared, practical, and trustworthy. Not trying to impress — trying to feel steady and ready for life.

---

### 2. The Maverick (Letter B)

| Attribute | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| **Energy** | Unrestricted, expressive, able to move freely through the world |
| **Presence** | Energetic, intriguing, hard to predict |
| **Confidence Signal** | Freedom, individuality, openness |
| **Style Identity** | Versatile and hard to pin down — labels feel limiting |
| **Shopping Pull** | Unusual details, statement items, things that feel different from the norm |
| **Trend Relationship** | Playful — enjoys bending or remixing trends on own terms |
| **Style Changes Because** | Resists repetition; craves freedom, novelty + movement |

> Driven by freedom, individuality, and self-expression. Resists being boxed in and sees fashion as a playground versus a rule book. Wardrobe evolves with curiosity, experimentation, and refusal to blend in.

---

### 3. The Architect (Letter C)

| Attribute | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| **Energy** | Intentional, composed, outer appearance reflects inner clarity |
| **Presence** | Focused, sharp, intentional |
| **Confidence Signal** | Competence, intention, clarity |
| **Style Identity** | Defined and purposeful — style has a clear through line |
| **Shopping Pull** | Clean lines, thoughtful construction, architectural shapes |
| **Trend Relationship** | Strategic — adopts trends selectively if they align with vision |
| **Style Changes Because** | Refines look over time, adjusting details rather than reinventing |

> Intentional, precise, and deeply aware of the power of presentation. Appreciates structure, clarity, and cohesion. Style is often refined and purposeful — every single choice is considered and calculated, nothing is accidental.

---

### 4. The Alchemist (Letter D)

| Attribute | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| **Energy** | Emotionally attuned to current mood, inner shifts, or creative state |
| **Presence** | Intense, layered, emotionally resonant |
| **Confidence Signal** | Emotional depth, creativity, internal movement |
| **Style Identity** | Fluid and evolving — style mirrors internal transformations |
| **Shopping Pull** | Texture, contrast, mood, depth — how something feels more than how it looks |
| **Trend Relationship** | Curious — explores trends as a form of self-expression or experimentation |
| **Style Changes Because** | Experiences life in phases; style evolves alongside inner world |

> Experiences style as an emotional and creative language. Moves through phases, moods, and transformation. Wardrobe reflects inner depth and personal evolution. Clothing becomes a tool for storytelling, exploration, and self-discovery.

---

### 5. The Sovereign (Letter E)

| Attribute | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| **Energy** | Fully aligned — outfit disappears and becomes an extension of self |
| **Presence** | Calm, self-possessed, centered |
| **Confidence Signal** | Alignment — nothing feels forced, everything feels natural |
| **Style Identity** | Integrated and whole — all parts allowed to coexist |
| **Shopping Pull** | Pieces that immediately feel right, even if hard to explain why |
| **Trend Relationship** | Detached — trends don't define them, no strong reaction to them |
| **Style Changes Because** | Style integrates many influences and shifts without losing coherence |

> Embodies integration and self-possession. Not chasing trends or identities — expressing a fully formed sense of self. Style feels effortless, grounded, and quietly powerful because it comes from alignment, not performance.

## Archetype-to-Service Relationship

The archetype system serves as a **discovery and alignment tool** for Danielle's styling services:

- The **archetype IS the product** — the quiz is just the discovery mechanism
- The quiz can be self-driven by the client OR administered by Danielle during a consultation
- Consultation purpose: use archetype knowledge to sell and align styling **services**
- Do NOT imply "book a consult to find your archetype" — that's backwards
- The flow is: **Discover archetype -> Shop the style -> Book services for deeper alignment**
