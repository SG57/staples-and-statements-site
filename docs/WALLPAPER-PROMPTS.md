# Archetype Detail Page — AI Wallpaper Generation Prompts

> **Purpose:** Full-viewport hero background images for each of the 12 archetype detail pages.
> **Requirements:** Landscape 16:9, atmospheric/cinematic, NO people in frame — pure setting/mood.
> **Output:** WebP, minimum 2400px wide, quality 85-90.
> **Naming:** `public/images/archetypes/{slug}-wallpaper.webp`
> **Last updated:** 2026-03-11

---

## Prompt Structure Notes

Each prompt follows a consistent formula:
1. **Setting/environment** — the physical space
2. **Lighting** — time of day, light quality, direction
3. **Key textures & materials** — what the eye touches
4. **Color temperature & palette** — dominant tones
5. **Atmosphere/mood** — the feeling the image evokes
6. **Technical direction** — camera angle, depth of field, style

All prompts include the suffix: `Cinematic photography, ultra-wide 16:9 aspect ratio, shallow depth of field with atmospheric haze, no people, no text, editorial luxury aesthetic.`

## Generation Notes

### Recommended Tools
- **Midjourney v6+** — Best for cinematic atmosphere and editorial lighting
- **DALL-E 3 (ChatGPT)** — Good for precise composition and object placement
- **Grok (xAI)** — Strong on mood and texture; test with these prompts
- **Gemini Imagen 3** — Good fallback for photorealistic interiors

### Post-Processing
1. Generate at maximum resolution available
2. Upscale to minimum 2400px wide (4K preferred for retina: 3840px)
3. Export as WebP at quality 85-90
4. Ensure the center-right area of the image has enough dark/simple space for text overlay (archetype name, icon, description)
5. Test with white text overlaid before finalizing — hero text must be legible

### Overlay Consideration
The detail page hero will overlay white text on the left or center-left. When reviewing generated images:
- Prefer compositions where the most complex visual detail is on the RIGHT side
- The LEFT or CENTER-LEFT should have softer, darker, or more uniform areas for text legibility
- The `text-bloom` CSS class and gradient overlays will help, but the base image composition matters

### Batch Generation Strategy
Generate 3-4 variants per archetype, then select the one with:
1. Best text overlay compatibility (dark/uniform area for text)
2. Most accurate mood match to the archetype energy
3. Strongest cinematic quality at full viewport scale
4. No distracting artifacts or uncanny elements
