# Service Page — AI Image Generation Prompts

> **Purpose:** Two sets of images for the Staples & Statements services page — service card banners and hero mosaic backgrounds.
> **Brand aesthetic:** Editorial luxury. Warm neutrals (charcoal #1a1613, cream #faf7f4, taupe #8c7e72, muted gold #b8977e). Cinematic lighting, fashion magazine quality. Vogue editorial meets warm California lifestyle.
> **Output format:** WebP, quality 85-90, minimum 2400px on the long edge.
> **Last updated:** 2026-03-20

---

## Prompt Structure Notes

Each prompt follows a consistent formula:
1. **Subject/scene** — the primary visual focus
2. **Lighting** — direction, quality, warmth
3. **Color palette** — warm neutrals anchored to the brand palette
4. **Composition notes** — relevant to how the image will be cropped/used
5. **Quality modifiers** — editorial, luxury, resolution, style

All prompts end with: `No text, no logos, no watermarks.`

## Generation Notes

### Recommended Tools
- **Midjourney v6+** — Best for cinematic atmosphere, editorial lighting, and fashion flat-lays
- **DALL-E 3 (ChatGPT)** — Good for precise object placement in flat-lays and still lifes
- **Grok (xAI)** — Strong on mood and texture; test with the moodier prompts (VIP, fabric swatches)
- **Gemini Imagen 3** — Good fallback for photorealistic interiors and product shots

### Aspect Ratio Summary
| Set | Orientation | Ratio | Midjourney Flag |
|---|---|---|---|
| Service cards | Portrait | 3:4 | `--ar 3:4` |
| Hero mosaic | Landscape | 4:3 | `--ar 4:3` |

### Post-Processing
1. Generate at maximum resolution available
2. Upscale to minimum 2400px on the long edge (4K preferred for retina)
3. Export as WebP at quality 85-90
4. **Service cards:** Test by cropping to a narrow horizontal strip (roughly 4:1) from the center — verify the subject is still identifiable
5. **Hero mosaic:** Test with white text overlaid at ~60% opacity gradient — verify legibility

### Color Consistency
All images should feel like they belong to the same brand. The dominant palette across every image should be:
- **Warm cream/ivory** (#faf7f4 vicinity) as the lightest tone
- **Muted warm gold** (#b8977e vicinity) for metallic/accent elements
- **Warm charcoal** (#1a1613 vicinity) for the darkest elements
- **Taupe/stone** (#8c7e72 vicinity) for mid-tones
- Controlled color pops only where the service demands it (color analysis swatches, seasonal palette cards)

### Batch Generation Strategy
Generate 3-4 variants per prompt, then select the one with:
1. Best crop compatibility (service cards must survive narrow cropping)
2. Best text overlay compatibility (hero mosaic images need dark/uniform areas)
3. Most accurate mood match to the specific service
4. Strongest editorial quality and brand coherence
5. No distracting AI artifacts, no uncanny elements, no visible text or logos
